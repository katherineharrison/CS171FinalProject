Pieces in the Harvard Art Museum's collection from 1800 to today.

The Harvard Art Museum, with its vast collection of works, serves as an ideal teaching tool for students, however, its very strength in size presents a daunting task when it comes to visualizing its capabilities.

Our website will allow students to view and sort pieces in the collection by region, date, color, artist, and movement, featuring a comparison tool so that students can visualize the collection in a more digestible way.

We will provide users with different tools with which to examine the collection in the hopes that we can better represent what the Harvard Art Museums have to offer.
